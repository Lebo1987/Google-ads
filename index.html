How We Calculate Revenue

Our estimation algorithm uses real-time data from trusted sources to provide accurate revenue calculations.

Revenue Formula:

Revenue = Traffic × CTR × CPC × Ad Placement Factor

Traffic: Monthly visitors from analytics sources
CTR (Click-Through Rate): Average percentage of users clicking ads
CPC (Cost-Per-Click): Estimated ad earnings per click
Ad Placement Factor: Adjustment based on ad location & visibility
